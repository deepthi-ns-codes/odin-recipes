Pulao

Home

Description

Pulao is a simple and flavorful rice dish cooked with spices, vegetables, and basmati rice. It is light, aromatic, and easy to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (chopped)
  • Green peas
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Wash and soak rice for 15–20 minutes.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pan.
  3. Add whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves) and sauté.
  4. Add onions and fry until golden.
  5. Add vegetables and cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add soaked rice and mix gently.
  7. Add water and salt, then bring to a boil.
  8. Cover and cook on low flame until rice is done.
  9. Fluff with a fork and serve hot.
