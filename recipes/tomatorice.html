Tomato Rice

Home

Description

Tomato rice is a South Indian style spicy and tangy rice dish made with tomatoes, spices, and cooked rice. It is quick to prepare and very flavorful.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 3 ripe tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 2 green chilies
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Curry leaves
  • Turmeric powder
  • Red chili powder
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add mustard seeds and cumin seeds, let them splutter.
  3. Add curry leaves, green chilies, and onions. Sauté until soft.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric, chili powder, and salt.
  6. Cook until tomatoes become soft and mushy.
  7. Add cooked rice and mix gently so spices coat the rice.
  8. Cook for 2–3 minutes and turn off the heat.
  9. Serve hot with curd or pickle.
